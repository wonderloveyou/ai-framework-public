# B2B AI Agency Strategy Skill

## Description
Complete strategy for building and marketing a B2B AI automation agency: positioning, service design, pricing, Tier-1 market entry, and growth frameworks. Based on analysis of 17 AI companies reaching $100M ARR, Sally AI, Velmi.ai cases, and global market research.

## When to Use
- Positioning a new AI automation agency
- Expanding AI agency to Tier-1 markets (US/UK/EU)
- Designing AI service offerings
- Pricing AI services
- Planning global go-to-market strategy
- Building AI agency growth playbook

## Process

### Step 1: Choose Your Wedge (Narrow Positioning)
"AI automation agency" = noise. Choose ONE narrow wedge:
- "We stop fake ad clicks draining your budget" (bot protection)
- "We cut qualified lead costs by 40% in 30 days" (AI SDR)
- "We replace manual SEO analysis with AI at 100+ projects scale" (AI SEO)

Formula: "We help [ICP] fix [expensive problem] without [objection] in [timeframe]"

Apply April Dunford's 5-component positioning:
1. Competitive alternatives (what they compare you to)
2. Unique attributes (what makes you different)
3. Value + proof (numbers, cases)
4. Best-fit customers (ICP)
5. Market category (where you play)

### Step 2: Design Service Lines
4 proven lines for AI agencies:
1. **AI Agents & Chatbots**: text + voice neuro-managers for lead qualification, cold calling, objection handling, auto-CRM
2. **Internal Process Automation**: smart scoring, document flow, reporting, logistics on autopilot
3. **SEO, GEO & Content**: neural networks for mass content creation (posts, articles, product descriptions, site optimization)
4. **Custom AI Solutions**: architecture design + turnkey build

Target: business owners, online schools, SaaS, agencies hitting hiring/budget ceiling, tired of human factor.

NOT for: idea-stage businesses with no budget.

### Step 3: Set Pricing (Price Against Labor, Not Software)
5 models:
1. **Implementation Fee**: $25-80K one-time
2. **Agent Licensing**: $2-5K/month recurring
3. **AI Audit Gateway**: $5K entry point
4. **Outcome-Based**: % of savings/growth delivered
5. **Platform Tiers**: SaaS-like monthly plans

Key rule: price against the LABOR COST you replace, not against software alternatives.

### Step 4: Build Sales Motion (6 Laws from 17 AI Companies at $100M ARR)
1. **Start With a Wedge That Prints Value** — narrow, measurable, undeniable
2. **Win the Buyer, the Market Follows** — one vertical, one ICP, dominate
3. **Domain-Expert GTM** Outperforms Generic Sales — hire from the industry
4. **Build Proof That Can't Be Argued With** — live demos on THEIR data, not samples
5. **Price Against Labor Cost** — if AI replaces $150K salary, $50K is a bargain
6. **Build Expansion Into Product Logic, Not Sales Motion** — product-led upsells

### Step 5: Sell (6 Sales Laws)
1. **Founders Sell Every Deal** Until the Motion Is Proven
2. **Demo Against Their Own Work**, Not Sample Data
3. **Willingness-to-Pay = Qualification Signal**, Not a Negotiation
4. **Paid Pilots** with Pre-Agreed Exit Conditions
5. **The Hardest Objector** Is Your Best Potential Champion
6. **Sell to the Practitioner First** — Let Them Pull Procurement

### Step 6: Enter Tier-1 Markets (90-Day Plan)
Weeks 1-2: choose niche + produce 3 case studies
Weeks 3-4: infrastructure (site, LinkedIn, email setup) + content
Weeks 5-8: target 200-400 ICP accounts, test 3-4 email variants
Weeks 9-12: optimize winners, produce 2-3 "hero cases"

Channels for Tier-1: LinkedIn (primary), Reddit (native value-first), email (compliant), communities (Pavilion, CMO Coffee Talk), HN (1/month, only if you have a tool)

### Step 7: Handle the AI Reality
- AI is "background" technology — sell OUTCOMES, not AI
- 96% of marketers use AI (2026) — it's hygiene minimum, not differentiator
- 30+ B2B founders: all use ChatGPT/Claude, all have n8n automations, all talk "agents" — but NONE truly implemented AI agents. The market is wide open.
- AI content quality levels: Text (worthless) → Expert-verified → Result-proven (gold standard)
- GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the new SEO — optimize for ChatGPT/Perplexity answers

## Key Principles
- Narrow Wedge > Broad Platform
- Proof Before Pitch — cases, live demos, real data
- Audit → Pilot → Retainer motion
- Founders sell until motion is proven
- Network effect is non-linear: 500 contacts → 1,500 reach. 2,500 contacts = 10x revenue
