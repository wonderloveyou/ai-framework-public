# B2B Content Strategy Skill

## Description
Structured B2B content strategy methodology: funnel design, customer segmentation, content prioritization, and measurement. Based on proven frameworks from "Сделаем" agency, Нескучные Финансы, and Roistat.

## When to Use
- Designing B2B content strategy from scratch
- Optimizing existing B2B funnel
- Prioritizing content efforts (what to do first)
- Building content measurement framework
- Planning B2B agency marketing

## Process

### Step 1: Map Customer Pathways
- Identify ALL paths customers take to your product (referral, search, ads, partners, etc.)
- For each path: what questions, pains, objections arise
- Hierarchy: customer pain → content topic → format → platform (NEVER reverse)

### Step 2: Segment Audience by Proximity to Purchase
1. Current clients (retention, LTV)
2. Hot leads (already searching, applying)
3. Warm leads (interested in the topic)
4. With pains (have problem, haven't connected to solution)
5. Cold (fit profile, not relevant now)

Priority is BOTTOM-UP. Start with segments 1-2, then 3-4, segment 5 = low priority.

### Step 3: Run Mini-Research Cycles
Don't spend 6 months on research. Mini-cycles per segment × per source:
- Sales team + CRM (how people formulate problems, objections)
- Current clients (how they came to purchase)
- Production/execution team (why clients churn)
- Potential clients (interviews by persona)

### Step 4: Design the Funnel
For each stage:
- Top (Awareness): educational content, tips, free materials → soft CTA
- Middle (Consideration): cases with results, webinars, demos → qualification
- Bottom (Decision): free audit/consultation, ROI calculator, pilot → hard CTA
- Post-sale (Retention): knowledge base, updates, community → retention

### Step 5: Apply the Traffic Light Model
- GREEN (Must Have): case studies, TG showcase, SEO, YouTube, lead magnets+funnel, networking
- YELLOW (Try after): personal brand, media for specialists, 2-3 articles on VC/Habr, partner programs
- RED (Don't): useful content for LPRs, VC.ru as blog, VK as platform, Dzen, cold traffic for courses

### Step 6: Set KPIs
- NOT subscribers or reach
- Primary: qualified leads → closed deals
- Secondary: associated conversions (visited case pages → submitted application)
- Qualitative: client surveys every 1-2 months

## Key Principles
1. Strategy comes from customer paths, NOT from formats or trends
2. Start from the BOTTOM of the funnel (cases, proof, trust)
3. Cases are the foundation of B2B content
4. KPI = clients, not subscribers
5. 80% educational, 20% promotional content
6. Ads come AFTER foundation (cases + offer) — otherwise it's budget drain
