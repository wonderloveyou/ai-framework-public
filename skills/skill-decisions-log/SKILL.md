# Decisions Log — Skill

## Goal
Record decisions so agents and humans can revisit why something was done a certain way.

## When to log
- Architecture decision made
- Tool/library chosen over alternatives
- Business rule established
- Workflow changed
- Design pattern adopted
- Trade-off accepted

## Format (append to decisions.md)
```md
## YYYY-MM-DD: [Title]

Context: what was the situation?
Decision: what was chosen?
Alternatives: what was considered and rejected?
Rationale: why this choice?
Consequences: what does this affect?
```
